The Crisis We're Solving

In Togo and across Africa, families face significant challenges in finding safe, affordable housing. Here's the story of how we're changing lives:

01

The Housing Crisis

More than 500,000 families in urban areas are without adequate housing. Traditional solutions can't keep up with the growing demand, leaving communities without options.

02

Prohibitive Costs

Traditional construction methods cost 3-5x an average annual salary. Building a home becomes an unreachable dream for most families, forcing them into inadequate housing.

03

Years of Waiting

With build times averaging 12-18 months, families spend years in temporary housing, paying rent while also trying to fund their future home construction.

04

The Energy Challenge

Only 45% of homes have reliable access to electricity. Power outages are common, lasting hours or days, disrupting work, education, and daily life.

05

Innovation Barriers

Entrepreneurs and remote workers struggle without dedicated workspaces. This limits economic growth and opportunities for young professionals to contribute to the economy.

But this isn't the end of the story... TACH is changing this narrative with innovative container homes that address each of these challenges.
